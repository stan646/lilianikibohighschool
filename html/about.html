About Lilian Kibo High School

Lilian Kibo High School is a boarding school for both girls & boys located in Goba, Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. We offer a balanced curriculum including Science, Arts and Business combinations.

Our Vision

To be a leading secondary school producing disciplined, competent, and responsible graduates.

Our Mission

  • Deliver high-quality academic programs
  • Develop students' character and leadership
  • Provide a safe and supportive boarding environment

Principal's Message

"Welcome to Lilian Kibo High School — where we prepare students for life through knowledge and integrity."
