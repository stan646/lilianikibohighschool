About Us

Lilian Kibo High School is a boarding school located in Goba, Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. We provide both boys and girls with quality secondary education.

Our Vision

To be a leading secondary school in Tanzania producing competent, disciplined, and responsible citizens.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that develops academic excellence, leadership skills, and moral values.
